Week 1 — Phase 1: Foundation
AIC / Right BC Correction Protocol

Your Left AIC / Right BC pattern means your left hip flexor complex is overactive, your right glute is inhibited, and your thorax rotates right. The warm-up below resets this before every session.

1
90/90 Hip Shift with Reach — Left
3 x 5 breaths. Shift to left, reach left arm up. Full exhale. Resets left AIC pattern.
2
Right Glute Max Activation — Clamshells
2 x 15 right side only. Band above knee. Own your right glute.
3
Left Adductor Squeeze + Exhale
Ball between knees, supine, 90 degrees. Squeeze + full exhale 5x. Left AF IR reset.
4
Supine Right BC — Right Arm Reach
Lie back, reach right arm overhead + exhale. 5 x 3 sec. Depresses right shoulder girdle.
5
Single Leg Glute Bridge — Right
2 x 12 right only. Drive heel, pause 2 sec. Reinforces right glute in functional position.

Progressive Overload System
1
Foundation and Motor Pattern
3-4 sets x 12-15 reps at 65-70% 1RM. Add 1 rep per set weekly until top of range, then add weight and reset. RPE target 6-7. Deload Week 4.
Weeks 1-4
2
Hypertrophy and Volume
4 sets x 8-12 reps at 70-75% 1RM. Drop sets on final set of compound movements. Track weekly tonnage. RPE target 7-8. Deload Week 8.
Weeks 5-8
3
Strength and Density

Knee Management Rules
⚠️
Pendulum Squat Depth
Max 90 degrees for weeks 1-4. Only increase depth if zero pain/swelling after sessions.
✅
Terminal Knee Extensions (TKEs)
2x20 before every leg session. Activates VMO and protects ACL graft.
🚫
No Deep Leg Press
Below 90 degrees adds excessive compressive force. Maintain 90 degrees or above always.
🔄
Hamstring Curl Before Extensions
Always do curls first. Reduces anterior tibial shear during leg extensions.
